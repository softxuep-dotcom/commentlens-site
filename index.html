Features

Turn comment sections into useful research.

CSV export

Collect comments from YouTube videos and Shorts, then export author, text, likes, replies, time, and comment links to CSV.

Question mining

Filter comments that contain questions so creators can find FAQ topics, objections, and content ideas faster.

Keyword analysis

Surface repeated words and phrases from the collected comments to identify audience language and recurring themes.

Buyer-intent detection

Highlight comments that mention price, links, buying, discounts, orders, or similar purchase signals.
